# Windows Settings (SystemSettings)

Known behavior and navigation paths:
- Personalization:
  - Changing between **Light Mode** and **Dark Mode** is located under the **Colors** section (Personalization > Colors), NOT under Themes.
  - Custom background, lock screen, fonts, and theme packages are under their respective sub-menus (Themes, Background, Lock screen).
- System Settings:
  - Display resolution, sound, notifications, and power settings are under System.
- Apps Settings:
  - Installed apps, default apps, and startup settings are under Apps.
